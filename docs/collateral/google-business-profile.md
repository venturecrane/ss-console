# Google Business Profile — SMD Services

Setup guide and ready-to-paste content for the SMD Services GBP listing.

---

## Step 1: Claim Your Profile

1. Go to [Google Business Profile Manager](https://business.google.com/)
2. Sign in with the Google account you want to own the listing (use your business Google account, not personal)
3. Click **Add your business to Google**
4. Search for "SMD Services" to make sure no listing already exists
5. If no existing listing, click **Add your business**
6. Select **Service-area business** (you go to clients, clients don't come to you)

**Verification:** Google will ask you to verify ownership. Options:

- **Phone/text** to the business number (fastest)
- **Email** to an @smd.services address
- **Video verification** (show business docs on camera)
- **Postcard** to your address (takes 5-7 days)

Go with phone or email if available. Video is fine if those aren't offered.

## Step 6: Weekly Posting Cadence

Post once a week. Rotate through these content types:

| Week | Type                    | Example                                                                                                                                 |
| ---- | ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | **Problem recognition** | "If your team texts you every time a decision needs to be made, it's not a people problem. There's just no process for them to follow." |
| 2    | **What we do**          | Describe a service or part of the engagement in plain language.                                                                         |
| 3    | **Local relevance**     | Tie to Phoenix business community, a vertical you're targeting, or a local observation.                                                 |
| 4    | **Credibility**         | Share an insight from 20 years of building software and operations. Not a brag. Just something useful.                                  |

**Post format:**

- 2-4 sentences. No hashtags.
- Include a CTA link to `https://smd.services/book` when relevant.
- Add a photo when you have one. Posts with photos get more visibility.

**Posting URL:** [Google Business Profile posts](https://business.google.com/) > Your business > Add update

## Step 7: Review Strategy

### Asking for Reviews

After each engagement, send a review request. GBP provides a short link:

1. Go to [Business Profile](https://business.google.com/)
2. Click **Ask for reviews** (or Profile > Share review form)
3. Copy the short link and send it to the client

**Template message:**

```
Hey [Name] — thanks again for working with us. If you have a minute,
a Google review would go a long way for us as a new business.
No pressure at all. Here's the link if you're up for it: [review link]
```

### Responding to Reviews

Respond to every review within 24 hours.

**Positive review response template:**

```
Thanks [Name]. It was great working with your team. Glad we could
help get things running smoother.
```

**Negative review response template:**

```
Thanks for the feedback [Name]. We take this seriously. Let's talk
about what happened — [phone/email].
```

Keep responses short, genuine, no marketing language.

## Step 8: Tracking

Check performance monthly at:
[GBP Performance Dashboard](https://business.google.com/) > Performance

Track these metrics:

- **Search queries** that triggered your listing
- **Profile views** (Search vs Maps)
- **Website clicks**
- **Direction requests**
- **Phone calls**
- **Booking clicks**

Screenshot monthly and save to `docs/metrics/` for trend tracking.
